Landmarks

Niagara Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder composed of three waterfalls. The massive Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls, and the smaller Bridal Veil Falls, straddling the U.S.-Canada border, known for immense water volume, thundering sound, misty spray, and vibrant rainbows, offering stunning views from surrounding parks and boat tours, and serving as a major source of hydroelectric power.

The CN Tower is Toronto's iconic 553.3-meter (1,815 ft) communication and observation tower, known for its stunning city views from observation decks, including a glass floor and revolving restaurant (360 Restaurant). It serves as a major telecommunications hub and a world-renowned tourist attraction with experiences like the high-altitude EdgeWalk for thrill-seekers, making it a symbol of Canada and a must-visit landmark for panoramic cityscapes and adrenaline rushes.
